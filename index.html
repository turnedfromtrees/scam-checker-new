📋 How to Use This Tool
  1. Open the suspicious email
  2. Click anywhere in the email body
  3. Press Ctrl+A (select all) then Ctrl+C (copy)
    💡 Or drag your mouse to select the entire email
  4. Paste it in the box above (Ctrl+V or right-click → Paste)
  5. Include the "From:" line if visible — it helps us check the sender!
    🔒 We don't store or send your email anywhere — everything stays on your computer

💡 Tip: The more you paste, the better we can analyze it!
